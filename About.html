GYEP (Gondolashu Youth Empowerment Programme) is Education Non-Profit Organisation Started in June 2021. Operating as a South African Registered Non Profit Company. It was Istabilished by youth from Nzhelele village in Limpopo. The Organisation is Open To anyone who has helping Spirit .The Organisation Has Been a Successful Projects for Years Offering Education Help To Local Students. Students Were Motivated, Assissted and Offered Goods and Services That Enhence Them a Better Future. Organisation is Equiped by Voluteeus from Goverment Workers, Tertiary Students and Members of Community. Assistance is Currently Offered To Learners From High Schools Around Vhembe District with Vision To Grow Around The Whole Of South Africa.
