We Are A Non-Profit Company Based In Northeren Part Of Limpopo (Vhembe District).The
Organisation was found to address issues faced by learners in today world.

GYEP (Gondolashu Youth Empowerment Programme) is Education Non-Profit Organisation Started in June 2021.The Organisation Has Been a Successful Projects for Years Offering Education Help To Location Students. Students were Motivated, Assissted and Offered Goods and Services That Enhence Them a Better future. Organisation is Equiped by Voluteeus from Goverment Workers, Tertiary Students and Members of Community. Assistance is Currently Offered To Learners From High Schools Around Vhembe District with Vision To Grow Around The Whole Of South Africa. With The Help From Principals, Teachers and Officals In the District we Achive A tremedus Job.
